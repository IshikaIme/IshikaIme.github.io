About Me

About Me

Hello, I'm Ishika Tarin, but you can call me Ime. I completed my undergraduate BSc. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET) in Dhaka, Bangladesh. I am always eager to learn new things and take on new challenges. I believe that each and every moment should be enjoyed, for this youth wont come twice. I am a fan of arts and also love to hangout. I love painting, dancing, singing, travelling and trying new food items. I also love to photograph each moment I spend enjoying.

Currently, I am serving as a Research Assistant in the Next-generation Computing (NeC) Research Group within the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at BUET, a position I have held since July 2024. In this role, I am actively involved in a significant research project funded by the Water Resources Planning Organization (WARPO) under the Ministry of Water Resources, Government of Bangladesh. The project is centered on applying advanced technologies to enhance groundwater data imputation and forecasting specifically for the North Western region of Bangladesh.
